# Quest Board Product

Quest Board is a tiny fictional project for people learning Kiro. It lets a user create a quest, choose its difficulty, and mark it complete without hiding the idea behind production-scale architecture.

## Audience

- Community members exploring a working Kiro workflow
- Developers trying Kiro for the first time
- Teams learning how steering and specs connect to source files
- Demo viewers who need a project they can understand in minutes

## Product goals

- Keep the Quest Board example deliberately small
- Make every Kiro artifact easy to inspect
- Use Kiro Atlas to make the workspace workflow visible
- Favor a clear teaching moment over extra features
