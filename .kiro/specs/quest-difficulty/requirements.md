# Quest Difficulty Requirements

## Choose a difficulty

Every quest has exactly one difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard. New quests accept a valid difficulty when they are created.

## Show a readable label

Consumers can turn the stored lowercase difficulty into a human-readable label for the interface.

## Preserve simple types

The difficulty values are represented by the `Difficulty` union in `demo/difficulty.ts` and used by `demo/quests.ts`.
